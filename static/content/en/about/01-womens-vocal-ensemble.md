# Echappees Sonores - Women's vocal ensemble

Born from a common passion for lyrical singing and a shared artistic requirement, the female vocal ensemble Échappées Sonores has established itself, since its creation in 2024, as a choral group seeking excellence in the heart of Paris. 

The choir was created on the initiative of Marilou Gonsseaume, choir director specializing in conducting at the Conservatoire à Rayonnement Régional de Paris (CRR de Paris), where she has trained with Pierre Louis de Laporte, a recognized figure in the French choral world. The fruit of a rigorous and passionate musical career, Marilou Gonsseaume is not at her beginnings: with several years of practice in choir conducting, she has already conducted concerts mixing choir and orchestra, thus developing precise gestures, a keen ear and an assertive artistic vision. It is driven by the desire to bring together female voices around an ambitious lyrical project that she founded Échappées Sonores — a name that says it all: escape through sound, freedom in music, invitation to inner journey.

# A noted debut in Berck-sur-Mer

Newly formed, Échappées Sonores was able to conquer its first audience. In May 2024, the ensemble gave its very first concert in Berck-sur-Mer, a town in Pas-de-Calais known for its attachment to culture and the sea.

This public premiere marked a founding moment for the choir: that of the meeting with the public, of the confrontation between the rehearsal work and the reality of the stage, confirmation that this artistic project deserved to come to life. For the choristers and their conductor alike, this first concert was far more than a simple performance: it sealed the group's identity, affirmed its ambitions, and opened the way to many future projects.

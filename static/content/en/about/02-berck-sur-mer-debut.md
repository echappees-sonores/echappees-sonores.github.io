# A noted debut in Berck-sur-Mer

Newly formed, Echappees Sonores had already won over its first audience. In May 2024, the ensemble gave its very first concert in Berck-sur-Mer, a town in Pas-de-Calais known for its connection to culture and the sea.

This first public appearance marked a founding moment for the choir: the encounter with an audience, the passage from rehearsal work to the reality of the stage, and the confirmation that this artistic project deserved to come to life. For the singers and their conductor alike, this first concert was far more than a simple performance: it sealed the group's identity, affirmed its ambitions, and opened the way to many projects still to come.

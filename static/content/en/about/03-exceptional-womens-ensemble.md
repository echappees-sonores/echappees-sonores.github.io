# A women's ensemble of exceptional standard

Echappees Sonores brings together around fifteen choristers, all women, all trained in some of the most prestigious musical institutions in France.

This shared foundation gives Echappees Sonores a strong vocal homogeneity and musical maturity. The singers share the same language, the same culture of lyrical singing, and the same attention to intonation, diction, phrasing and expression. This is not simply a group of singers gathering to sing together: it is a collective of trained artists choosing to place their voices and their individual paths at the service of a shared project, in a constant search for sound and for a mature, assured women's ensemble.

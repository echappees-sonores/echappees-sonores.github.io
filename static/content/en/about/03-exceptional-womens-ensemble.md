# A women's ensemble of an ambitious level

Échappées Sonores brings together around fifteen choristers, all women, all from prestigious musical groups.

This shared foundation gives Echappees Sonores a strong vocal homogeneity and musical maturity. The choristers share the same language, the same culture of lyrical singing, and the same demands regarding intonation, diction, phrasing and expression. It is not simply a group of singers who come to sing together - it is a collective of trained artists who chose to place their voices and their individual journeys at the service of a shared project, in a perpetual search for sound to tend towards a female ensemble with mature and assertive voices.

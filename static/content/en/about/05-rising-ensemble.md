# An ensemble on the rise

Echappees Sonores is a young but already solid ensemble, carried by singers whose training and artistic commitment leave no doubt as to the seriousness of the project. Based in Paris, at the crossroads of an intense musical life and a network of exceptional talents, the ensemble has every opportunity to quickly become a reference in the world of high-level women's choral singing.

Following Echappees Sonores means accompanying an artistic adventure as it is being built, with the certainty that the most beautiful pages of this story are still to be written.

# An ambitious ensemble in the making

Échappées Sonores is a young but already solid ensemble, led by singers whose training and artistic commitment leave no doubt as to the seriousness of the project. Based in Paris, at the crossroads of an intense, stimulating musical life and a network of exceptional talents, the ensemble aims to find its place in the world of high-level female choral singing. 

Following Échappées Sonores means accompanying an artistic adventure in the making, with the certainty that the most beautiful pages of this story have yet to be written.

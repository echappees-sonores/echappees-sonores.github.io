# Elevation

**2025/26 season**

There is something in French music of the Belle Epoque that seems to escape time: a grace, a lightness and a depth that pass through the century without growing old.

Tonight, fifteen young singers invite you to follow them along this path. From the playful joy of Chabrier to the luminous serenity of Honegger, through the tenderness of Faure, the impressionist breath of Debussy and the fervor of Poulenc, the program traces an arc from life toward what lies beyond it. It is precisely there, in that space between the world and the unsayable, that elevation is born.
